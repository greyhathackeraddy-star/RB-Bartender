🥃 Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is a timeless whiskey cocktail known for its strong, smooth and slightly sweet flavor. It is considered the original cocktail.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
USA 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Rocks Glass
Base Spirit:
Bourbon / Rye Whiskey
Flavor:
Strong, Sweet, Bitter

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 Sugar Cube
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Few drops Water
  • Ice Cubes
  • Orange Peel (Garnish)
  • Cherry (Optional)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Place sugar cube in glass.
2️⃣ Add bitters and a few drops of water.
3️⃣ Muddle until sugar dissolves.
4️⃣ Fill glass with large ice cubes.
5️⃣ Pour whiskey over ice.
6️⃣ Stir gently for 20–30 seconds.
7️⃣ Garnish with orange peel.

Garnish

  • Orange Peel Twist
  • Cherry (optional)

Bartender Notes

Old Fashioned is all about balance. Use good quality whiskey and don’t over-muddle. The orange oil from peel is the key aroma.
