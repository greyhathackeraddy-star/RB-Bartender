🍯 Hot Toddy

The Hot Toddy is a warm cocktail made with whiskey, honey, lemon, and hot water. It is soothing, relaxing, and often used as a winter comfort drink.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
United Kingdom 🇬🇧
Glassware:
Mug / Heatproof Glass
Base Spirit:
Whiskey
Taste:
Warm • Sweet • Citrus

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Whiskey
  • 1–2 tbsp Honey
  • 20 ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Hot Water
  • Cinnamon Stick (optional)
  • Lemon Slice (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add whiskey into a heatproof mug.
2️⃣ Add honey and lemon juice.
3️⃣ Pour hot water over mixture.
4️⃣ Stir until honey dissolves.
5️⃣ Add cinnamon stick (optional).
6️⃣ Garnish with lemon slice.
7️⃣ Serve warm immediately.

Garnish

  • Lemon Slice
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Clove (optional)

Bartender Notes

Don’t boil whiskey directly — always add hot water first. Great for cold weather and soothing sore throat.
