🥃 Whiskey Sour

The Whiskey Sour is a classic cocktail made with whiskey, lemon juice, and sugar syrup. It is refreshing, tangy, and smooth — often topped with foam.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
USA 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Rocks Glass
Base Spirit:
Bourbon / Whiskey
Taste:
Sweet • Sour • Smooth

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Whiskey
  • 25 ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 15 ml Sugar Syrup
  • Optional: Egg White (for foam)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Cherry (Garnish)
  • Orange Slice (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add whiskey, lemon juice, and sugar syrup into a shaker.
2️⃣ Add ice cubes.
3️⃣ Dry shake first (if using egg white).
4️⃣ Shake again with ice for 10–15 seconds.
5️⃣ Strain into a rocks glass.
6️⃣ Top with foam if used egg white.
7️⃣ Garnish with cherry and orange slice.

Garnish

  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Orange Slice
  • Lemon Peel (optional)

Bartender Notes

Egg white gives a silky foam texture, but you can skip it for a simpler version. Always use fresh lemon juice for best balance.
