🍷 Sangria (Red & White Wine)

Sangria is a refreshing Spanish wine punch made with fresh fruits, citrus, and wine. It can be made with red wine or white wine depending on preference.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Spain 🇪🇸
Glassware:
Wine Glass / Pitcher
Base:
Red or White Wine
Taste:
Fruity • Sweet • Refreshing

Ingredients

  • 750 ml Red or White Wine
  • 1 Apple (sliced)
  • 1 Orange (sliced)
  • 1 Lemon (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 100 ml Orange Juice
  • Soda Water (optional)
  • Ice Cubes

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Slice all fresh fruits into a pitcher.
2️⃣ Add sugar and orange juice.
3️⃣ Pour red or white wine.
4️⃣ Mix gently and refrigerate for 1–2 hours.
5️⃣ Add ice before serving.
6️⃣ Top with soda water (optional).
7️⃣ Serve chilled in wine glasses.

Garnish

  • Orange Slice
  • Apple Slice
  • Mint Leaves (optional)

Bartender Notes

The longer Sangria rests, the better the flavor. Always chill before serving for best taste.
