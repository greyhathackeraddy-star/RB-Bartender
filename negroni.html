🍊 Negroni

The Negroni is a classic Italian cocktail made with equal parts gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth. It is strong, bitter, and perfectly balanced.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Italy 🇮🇹
Glassware:
Rocks Glass
Base Spirit:
Gin
Taste:
Bitter • Sweet • Strong

Ingredients

  • 30 ml Gin
  • 30 ml Campari
  • 30 ml Sweet Vermouth
  • Ice Cubes
  • Orange Slice / Peel (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Fill a rocks glass with ice cubes.
2️⃣ Pour 30 ml gin into the glass.
3️⃣ Add 30 ml Campari.
4️⃣ Add 30 ml sweet vermouth.
5️⃣ Stir gently until well chilled.
6️⃣ Garnish with orange peel or slice.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Orange Peel Twist
  • Orange Slice (optional)

Bartender Notes

Negroni is all about balance — keep the equal ratio perfect. Use quality gin and stir properly to avoid over-dilution.
