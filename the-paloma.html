🍊 Paloma

The Paloma is a classic Mexican cocktail made with tequila and grapefruit soda. It is light, fizzy, and more refreshing than a Margarita for many people.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Mexico 🇲🇽
Glassware:
Highball Glass
Base Spirit:
Tequila
Taste:
Citrus • Fizzy • Refreshing

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Tequila
  • 120 ml Grapefruit Soda
  • 15 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Salt (optional rim)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Grapefruit Slice (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Rim glass with salt (optional).
2️⃣ Fill glass with ice cubes.
3️⃣ Add tequila and lime juice.
4️⃣ Top with grapefruit soda.
5️⃣ Stir gently.
6️⃣ Garnish with grapefruit slice.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Grapefruit Slice
  • Salt Rim (optional)
  • Lime Wedge

Bartender Notes

Use fresh grapefruit soda or juice + soda water mix for better flavor. It’s a lighter alternative to Margarita.
