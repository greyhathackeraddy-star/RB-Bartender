🍋 Daiquiri

The Daiquiri is a classic Cuban cocktail made with rum, lime juice, and sugar. It is light, refreshing, and perfectly balanced between sweet and sour.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Cuba 🇨🇺
Glassware:
Cocktail Glass
Base Spirit:
White Rum
Taste:
Sweet • Sour • Refreshing

Ingredients

  • 60 ml White Rum
  • 25 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 15 ml Sugar Syrup
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lime Wheel (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add white rum, lime juice, and sugar syrup into a shaker.
2️⃣ Add ice cubes.
3️⃣ Shake well for 10–15 seconds.
4️⃣ Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
5️⃣ Adjust sweetness if needed.
6️⃣ Garnish with lime wheel.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Lime Wheel
  • Lime Zest (optional)

Bartender Notes

Always use fresh lime juice for best taste. The balance between sweet and sour is the key to a perfect Daiquiri.
