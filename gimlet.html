🍋 Gimlet

The Gimlet is a classic gin cocktail made with lime juice and sweetener. It is crisp, refreshing, and extremely easy to drink.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
United Kingdom 🇬🇧
Glassware:
Martini Glass
Base Spirit:
Gin
Taste:
Sour • Sweet • Fresh

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Gin
  • 20 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 15 ml Sugar Syrup (or Rose’s Lime Cordial)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lime Wheel (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add gin, lime juice, and sugar syrup into a shaker.
2️⃣ Add ice cubes.
3️⃣ Shake well for 10–15 seconds.
4️⃣ Strain into a chilled martini glass.
5️⃣ Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
6️⃣ Garnish with lime wheel.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Lime Wheel
  • Lime Zest (optional)

Bartender Notes

Use fresh lime juice for best flavor. The Gimlet is all about simplicity — don’t overcomplicate it.
