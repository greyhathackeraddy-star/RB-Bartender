🍹 Long Island Iced Tea (LIIT)

LIIT is a powerful cocktail made with vodka, gin, rum, tequila, triple sec, and cola. It looks like iced tea but is much stronger.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
USA 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Highball Glass
Base Spirit:
Multiple Spirits
Taste:
Strong • Sweet • Citrus

Ingredients

  • 15 ml Vodka
  • 15 ml Gin
  • 15 ml White Rum
  • 15 ml Tequila
  • 15 ml Triple Sec
  • 25 ml Lemon Juice
  • Top with Cola
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lemon Slice (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
2️⃣ Add vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and triple sec.
3️⃣ Add fresh lemon juice.
4️⃣ Stir gently to mix spirits.
5️⃣ Top with cola.
6️⃣ Garnish with lemon slice.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Lemon Slice
  • Mint (optional)

Bartender Notes

LIIT is very strong because of multiple spirits. Always balance with lemon juice and cola.
