🍸 Martini

The Martini is one of the most iconic cocktails in the world. Made with gin and dry vermouth, it is known for its elegant, crisp and sophisticated character.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
United States 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Martini Glass
Base Spirit:
Gin
Garnish:
Olive or Lemon Twist

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Gin
  • 10 ml Dry Vermouth
  • Ice Cubes
  • Olive or Lemon Twist (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes.
2️⃣ Add 60 ml gin.
3️⃣ Add 10 ml dry vermouth.
4️⃣ Stir gently for 20–30 seconds until chilled.
5️⃣ Strain into a chilled martini glass.
6️⃣ Garnish with an olive or lemon twist.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Green Olive
  • Lemon Twist

Bartender Notes

A classic Martini is stirred, not shaken. Stirring keeps the cocktail crystal clear and silky. The ratio of gin to vermouth can be adjusted depending on guest preference, from dry to extra dry.
