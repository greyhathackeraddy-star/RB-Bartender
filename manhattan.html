🥃 Manhattan

The Manhattan is a classic whiskey cocktail made with rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters. It is strong, smooth, and perfectly balanced.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
USA 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Cocktail Glass
Base Spirit:
Rye Whiskey / Bourbon
Taste:
Strong • Sweet • Bitter

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Rye Whiskey
  • 30 ml Sweet Vermouth
  • 2–3 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Ice Cubes
  • Maraschino Cherry (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes.
2️⃣ Add rye whiskey.
3️⃣ Add sweet vermouth.
4️⃣ Add bitters.
5️⃣ Stir gently for 20–30 seconds.
6️⃣ Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
7️⃣ Garnish with cherry.

Garnish

  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Orange Peel (optional)

Bartender Notes

Always stir, never shake — this keeps the texture smooth and elegant. Use good quality whiskey for best flavor.
