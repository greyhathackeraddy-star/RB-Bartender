🍸 Margarita

The Margarita is one of the world's most popular tequila cocktails. It combines tequila, orange liqueur and fresh lime juice for a perfect balance of sweet, sour and strong flavors.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Mexico 🇲🇽
Glassware:
Margarita Glass
Base Spirit:
Tequila
Garnish:
Lime Wheel & Salt Rim

Ingredients

  • 50 ml Tequila
  • 25 ml Triple Sec (Orange Liqueur)
  • 25 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Ice Cubes
  • Salt (for rim)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Rim the glass with salt.
2️⃣ Fill a shaker with ice cubes.
3️⃣ Add tequila.
4️⃣ Add triple sec.
5️⃣ Add fresh lime juice.
6️⃣ Shake vigorously for 10–15 seconds.
7️⃣ Strain into the prepared glass.
8️⃣ Garnish with a lime wheel.

Garnish

  • Lime Wheel
  • Salt Rim

Bartender Notes

A proper Margarita should have a balanced sweet and sour profile. Always use fresh lime juice instead of bottled juice for the best taste. The salt rim enhances the citrus and tequila flavors.
