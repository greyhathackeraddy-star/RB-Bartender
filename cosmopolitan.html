🍸 Cosmopolitan

The Cosmopolitan is a modern classic cocktail made with vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and fresh lime juice. It is light, fruity, and elegantly pink.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
USA 🇺🇸
Glassware:
Martini Glass
Base Spirit:
Vodka
Taste:
Sweet • Tart • Fruity

Ingredients

  • 45 ml Vodka
  • 15 ml Triple Sec
  • 30 ml Cranberry Juice
  • 10 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lime Twist / Orange Peel (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice into a shaker.
2️⃣ Add ice cubes.
3️⃣ Shake well for 10–15 seconds.
4️⃣ Strain into a chilled martini glass.
5️⃣ Adjust color/taste if needed.
6️⃣ Garnish with lime twist.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Lime Twist
  • Orange Peel (optional)
  • Cranberry (optional)

Bartender Notes

Shake well to get a light frothy texture and proper blending of citrus and cranberry. Use fresh lime juice for best flavor.
