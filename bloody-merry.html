🍅 Bloody Mary

The Bloody Mary is a classic savory cocktail made with vodka, tomato juice, and spices. It is spicy, refreshing, and often served as a brunch drink.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
France / USA 🇫🇷🇺🇸
Glassware:
Highball Glass
Base Spirit:
Vodka
Taste:
Savory • Spicy • Tangy

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Vodka
  • 120 ml Tomato Juice
  • 15 ml Lemon Juice
  • Worcestershire Sauce (few drops)
  • Hot Sauce (to taste)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ice Cubes
  • Celery Stick (Garnish)
  • Olives (Garnish)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add vodka and tomato juice into a glass or shaker.
2️⃣ Add lemon juice and sauces.
3️⃣ Add salt, pepper, and hot sauce.
4️⃣ Stir or shake gently.
5️⃣ Pour into a tall glass with ice.
6️⃣ Garnish with celery and olives.
7️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Celery Stick
  • Olives
  • Lemon Wedge

Bartender Notes

Adjust spice level based on preference. The Bloody Mary is all about customization. Great as a brunch or hangover cocktail.
