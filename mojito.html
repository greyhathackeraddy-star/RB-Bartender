🍸 Mojito

The Mojito is a famous Cuban highball made with white rum, fresh lime juice, mint leaves, sugar and soda water. It is refreshing, light and one of the most popular cocktails in the world.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Cuba 🇨🇺
Glassware:
Highball Glass
Base Spirit:
White Rum
Garnish:
Mint Sprig & Lime Wedge

Ingredients

  • 60 ml White Rum
  • 20 ml Fresh Lime Juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 8–10 Mint Leaves
  • Soda Water
  • Ice Cubes

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add mint leaves and sugar into the glass.
2️⃣ Add fresh lime juice.
3️⃣ Gently muddle the mint to release its aroma.
4️⃣ Fill the glass with ice cubes.
5️⃣ Pour 60 ml white rum.
6️⃣ Top up with soda water.
7️⃣ Stir gently using a bar spoon.
8️⃣ Garnish with mint sprig and lime wedge.

Garnish

  • Fresh Mint Sprig
  • Lime Wedge

Bartender Notes

Do not over-muddle mint leaves. Over-muddling can make the cocktail bitter. Always use fresh mint and fresh lime juice for the best flavor.
