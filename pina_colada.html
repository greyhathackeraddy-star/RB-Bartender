🍍 Piña Colada

The Piña Colada is a sweet and creamy tropical cocktail made with pineapple, coconut cream and rum. It is one of the most famous beach drinks in the world.

Cocktail Information

Origin:
Puerto Rico 🇵🇷
Glassware:
Hurricane Glass / Tall Glass
Base Spirit:
White Rum
Style:
Frozen / Blended

Ingredients

  • 60 ml White Rum
  • 90 ml Pineapple Juice
  • 30 ml Coconut Cream
  • 1 cup Crushed Ice
  • Pineapple Slice (Garnish)
  • Cherry (Optional)

Method (Step by Step)

1️⃣ Add white rum, pineapple juice and coconut cream into a blender.
2️⃣ Add crushed ice.
3️⃣ Blend until smooth and creamy.
4️⃣ Pour into a chilled glass.
5️⃣ Adjust thickness if needed (add ice or juice).
6️⃣ Serve immediately.

Garnish

  • Pineapple Slice on rim
  • Cherry on top
  • Paper Umbrella (optional)

Bartender Notes

For best taste, always use fresh pineapple juice and chilled coconut cream. You can also make it extra thick for a smoothie-style tropical drink.
